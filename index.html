Recharge Bootcamp

Why Recharge Bootcamp?

Do you want to start the new year with clarity and confidence? Everyone dreams of greater profit, meaningful impact, and a life filled with purpose—but only a select few turn those dreams into reality.

Recharge Bootcamp 2025 is your gateway to making it happen. This exclusive event is designed to refuel your energy, reignite your passion, and equip you with a clear roadmap to achieve your biggest goals.

Whether you’re a business leader, an aspiring entrepreneur, a student, or a professional in any industry, Recharge Bootcamp is where you’ll reflect on your 2024 journey, refine your strategies, and set actionable, results-driven goals for 2025.

Guided by a lineup of successful leaders and innovators, you’ll discover how to unlock new opportunities, cultivate a success-driven mindset, and build meaningful connections with like-minded individuals.

Speakers' Bio


Joy Israel

7 Figure Freelancer and Business Builder

Joy Israel is a results-oriented freelancer, network marketing expert, and business strategist dedicated to empowering individuals to achieve financial independence.

With over a year of consistent mentorship, Joy has successfully trained and guided aspiring freelancers and network marketers, helping them transform into thriving professionals. Passionate about building success stories, Joy continues to inspire and lead a community of entrepreneurs, fostering sustainable growth and lasting impact.


Adeoye Oyekola

Award-Winning Digital Marketer & Visual Designer

Adeoye Oyekola is an award-winning Digital Marketer, Visual Designer, and the founder of Techvations and Sentrads. With over 8 years of expertise, he has transformed businesses through innovative paid media strategies, creative design, and cutting-edge web solutions.

A true industry leader, Adeoye’s work has earned him numerous accolades, including the prestigious Osun Awards in 2020, recognizing his outstanding contributions to digital marketing. Known for his data-driven approach and passion for design, he’s helped countless brands grow and thrive in an increasingly competitive digital space.

As a mentor and freelance coach, Adeoye empowers others to reach their full potential, blending creativity, technical expertise, and actionable insights. His vision, dedication, and proven track record make him a sought-after speaker and thought leader in the digital marketing world.


Pst. K.P. Oladosu

Pastor & Founder, Kundos Solutions

A multifaceted leader, Oladosu Paul Kehinde is a seasoned Software Developer, Marketing Consultant, and passionate Pastor. As the founder of Kundos Solutions, he has empowered numerous individuals with coding skills and career principles, transforming lives and fostering growth.

Through his ministerial platform, MKC, Oladosu has blessed countless lives by sharing the word of God with wisdom and compassion. His unique blend of technical expertise, entrepreneurial spirit, and spiritual leadership makes him a sought-after voice in personal development, career growth, and faith.

At the Recharge Bootcamp, Oladosu will inspire and equip you with practical insights on leveraging technology, marketing, and faith to propel your personal and professional growth. Get ready to be empowered and recharged!


Olawuni Emmanuel K

Software Engineer || Founder, Techifice

Olawuni Emmanuel Kayode (OK Emmanuel) is a visionary technologist, innovator, and personal development expert dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations to thrive in the digital age. As the founder of Techifice, a cutting-edge technology solutions company, he has established himself as a leading authority in leveraging technology to drive innovation, growth, and transformation.

With a strong background in technology and innovation, OK Emmanuel has built a reputation as a sought-after thought leader, speaker, and consultant. He has inspired countless individuals and organizations to harness the power of technology to achieve their goals and realize their potential.
